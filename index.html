Collective Wins - Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 2025

Introduction

Collective Wins is a group habit tracker app that helps individuals and groups track goals, build routines, and celebrate progress together. This privacy policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Email or username used to sign in.
  • Habit Data: Goals and progress you choose to track.
  • Device Data: Basic info automatically collected by Firebase (such as crash logs).

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the app experience. We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase services.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at: youremail@example.com.
